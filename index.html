Budget College Dining

Locations Around Campus

The struggle to find affortable food on campus is real! If you are looking for a cheap bite to eat, you found the right place. On this page, you can find plenty of tasty options to choose from. If you have a strict budget, don't worry! All choices on this blog are designed for you.

Chick-Fill-A

Chick-fil-A is the most popular dining spot for college students. Students appreciate the convenience of having Chick-Fil-A on campus because it offers high-quality food at a reasonable price. Many students eat at the Chick-Fil-A routinely. It is open 8am-9pm every day but Sunday.

Wendy's

Wendy's offers a solid fast-food experience with their signature square patties. The fries are decent, though not always as hot or crispy as they should be. Service speed can vary depending on the time of day. Overall, Wendy's is a reliable choice when you want something tasty and affordable. It is open 24/7, and its located nearby campus.

Sheetz

Sheetz is known for its touchscreen ordering system, which lets you customize everything from subs and wraps. The food is affordable, and the store is open 24/7. While it's not gourmet dining, it's a popular stop for busy college students. Its just outside of campus.

Did you know?

Some resteraunts around campus support on-campus meal plans. Ask a cashier for more information!

Quick Tips

  • Avoid going to resteraunts during peak hours for faster service.
  • Ask about student discounts, especially at resteraunts that don't support meal plans.
  • Always travel with friends when walking the streets at night!

Fun Fact

On-campus resteraunts are open to non-students. Dine with your visiting friends and family!
